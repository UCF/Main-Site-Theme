College of Medicine

At the Heart of Medical City

Established in 2006, the UCF College of Medicine is one of the first U.S. medical schools in decades to be built from the ground up. As a new medical school, UCF’s program epitomizes innovation, high-tech learning tools and a pioneering spirit to educate young doctors and scientists in a new and better way for the 21st century. Orlando's Central Florida location gives you easy access to many locations. Nature-enthusiasts can enjoy nearby miles of white-sand beaches, lagoons, barrier islands and nature preserves.
