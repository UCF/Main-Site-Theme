Nursing

The College of Nursing makes earning a nursing degree flexible, convenient and affordable with regional campuses and online programs. You can attend face-to-face "lock-step" programs in Orlando and on select regional campuses—and registered nurses (R.N.) can earn bachelor’s, master’s, Ph.D. and select DNP degrees online.

The BSN, MSN, DNP and post-graduate APRN certificate programs at UCF are accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education.

Visit nursing.ucf.edu

Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN), Master of Science in Nursing (MSN) and Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) programs are nationally accredited by the Commission of Collegiate Nursing Education.
Top4%
The college's baccalaureate students rank among the top 4% nationally on the NCLEX-RN license exam, routinely exceed state and national averages.
U.S. News & World Report has ranked the college as one of the nation's "Best Online Graduate Nursing Programs" and "Best Online Graduate Nursing Programs for Veterans."
10
The nationally recognized faculty includes 10 fellows of national nursing organizations and an advanced health care simulation educator, one of only 18 certified in the U.S.
The college pioneers innovative simulation technology to train students in state-of-the-art patient care techniques.
1st
The college is the first in Florida to offer an online RN to BSN degree, an online Ph.D. for nurse scientists and a Nursing and Healthcare Simulation graduate program.
