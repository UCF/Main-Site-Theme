Business

With more than 8,000 students, we offer undergraduate and graduate students an innovative business education in a high-tech atmosphere. We believe the best education happens when people from diverse backgrounds and experiences come together and have a conversation. The faculty and staff encourage students to become risk-takers, communicators and collaborators, problem solvers and data-driven decision makers. We are the only AACSB International accredited undergraduate and graduate college of business in Orlando. Fewer than 5 percent of business school programs worldwide have earned this accreditation, which requires strict adherence to rigorous standards of excellence.
