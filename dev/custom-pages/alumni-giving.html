What it means to be a Knight

Faculty and students make a university great. Research and education — in turn — help make our society a better place for everyone. But who ignites the spark that starts it all?

For UCF, it started when 89 donors came forward with a $1 million donation to secure the land that is now home to one of our nation's largest universities. Philanthropy powers the spark that created UCF, and now we have embarked on an unprecedented campaign to raise $500 million.

By leveraging the remarkable power of scale, we are challenging long-held assumptions about the role and scope of public higher education. And with philanthropic support, we are poised to emerge as the first in a new category of university, uniquely suited to meet the challenges and pursue the opportunities of our rapidly changing world.

Will you help ignite the future?

"UCF provided the challenge and laid the foundation I needed to become a lifelong learner, which I feel is one of the most valuable gifts anyone can receive."
David Bettner, '03, Words With Friends Co-Creator
