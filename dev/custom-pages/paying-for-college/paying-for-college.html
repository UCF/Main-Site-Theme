Student Debt and Affordability as Told by Students

At UCF, we think differently

A college degree is a critical investment. But don’t let the pressure of tuition, financial aid and scholarships stress you out. UCF is here to help you find opportunities to pay for classes, cover your cost of living, and even find a few extra bucks for fun.

Access and Affordability

The story of student debt has grabbed national headlines, but most news reports don’t tell the whole story. Compared to the average debt incurred by students of for-profit institutions, most UCF graduates face a far brighter future thanks to the university’s strong educational value and programs focused on fiscal responsibility and financial freedom.

Need help paying for school?

A college degree should create a lifetime of opportunities, not a lifetime of debt. At UCF, 48 percent of students graduate without any educational debt. Compare that to 33 percent nationally and you can see why UCF is gaining interest — in a good way.
