At UCF, the second-largest university in the U.S., bigger is better. Being big means more access for people of any age who might not otherwise get an opportunity to earn a high-quality college degree. Being big attracts experts from around the world to conduct innovative research and pass their knowledge on to students. And being big creates positive, lasting impact on the Central Florida community and beyond.
