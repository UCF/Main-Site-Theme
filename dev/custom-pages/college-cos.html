Innovative PTSD research: UCF REStores is an innovative clinical research center dedicated to the study of all facets of anxiety, trauma and posttraumatic stress disorder and is committed to helping active military, veterans and first responders. With help from a grant from the Department of Defense, we are developing intensive new PTSD treatment programs that are proving to be extremely effective — often on patients who have struggled with traditional approaches.
Marine Turtle Research Group – one of the oldest sea turtle research programs (30 years) and has one of the longest in-water research programs in the world. Monitors up to 40km of coastline of the most important nesting beaches in the Western Hemisphere. Provided first satellite tracks of baby turtles in the open ocean and contributed to post oil spill assessments of sea turtles in the Gulf of Mexico, establishing UCF as a RESTORE Act Center of Excellence.
Sustainable Coastal Systems Initiative – One of the first programs in the nation to employ a broad, interdisciplinary approach to address the complex environmental, economic and social problems that threaten coastal communities.
