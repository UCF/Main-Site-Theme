Business Administration

With more than 8,000 students, the college offers undergraduate and graduate students innovative thinking in a high-tech atmosphere. We are the only accredited undergraduate and graduate college of business in Orlando. All of our undergraduate and graduate programs are accredited by AACSB International. This represents the highest standard of achievement for business schools worldwide. Less than 5% of business school programs worldwide have earned this accreditation, which requires strict adherence to rigorous standards of excellence.
