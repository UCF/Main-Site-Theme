Unleash your potential. We can help.

UCF is for you if you're looking for a modern, energetic and alive campus built with you in mind. We offer 210 degree programs from faculty who care in one of the most vibrant cities in the world.

Whether you're a freshman, a transfer student, seeking a graduate degree or returning for continuing education, we value dedication to community and individual growth, in addition to academic excellence.

What type of student are you?

[full-width-image classes="overlay hidden-xs" filename="undergraduate-card" container="false"][/full-width-image]

Undergraduate

You're a freshman (first time in college/FTIC*) seeking an undergraduate degree.
[full-width-image classes="overlay hidden-xs" filename="transfer-card" container="false"][/full-width-image]

Transfer

You've completed 12+ credit hours of college course work after high school and are looking to transfer to UCF.
[full-width-image classes="overlay hidden-xs" filename="online-card" container="false"][/full-width-image]

Online

You're an undergraduate or graduate student interested in earning your degree online.
[full-width-image classes="overlay hidden-xs" filename="graduate-card" container="false"][/full-width-image]

Graduate

You've got a bachelor's degree or higher, seeking a master's degree or doctorate from UCF.
[full-width-image classes="overlay hidden-xs" filename="international-card" container="false"][/full-width-image]

International

You're a non-U.S. citizen seeking an undergraduate or graduate degree from UCF.
[full-width-image classes="overlay hidden-xs" filename="online-card" container="false"][/full-width-image]

Continuing Education

You're seeking professional advancement, certification training or personal growth.
* Also includes students applying with any dual enrollment credit during high school, event those concurrently earning an AA degree and a high school diploma. View the State University System Board of Governors definition of a First Time in College applicant. [sections-menu selector=".admissions-section"]
